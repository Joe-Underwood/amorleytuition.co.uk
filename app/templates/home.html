About Me

My name's Adam, and I'm a CELTA qualified ESL teacher and Maths tutor from Cambridge, UK with over 400 hours of experience.

I love travelling and have taught English all over the world, to adults in England and children in Peru. Because of my experience I always aim to teach you practical language skills to help you survive in an English speaking country.

Whatever your goals I can help you attain confidence and fluency in written and spoken English.

I look forward to hearing from you!

  • MSc (Hons) Mathematics (1st Class) University of Nottingham
  • CELTA Qualified
  • 400+ Hours of Experience

Lessons

Maths:

I tend to start with a 5 minute review of the previous lesson, followed by an introduction to today's topic. We then work through problems of increasing difficulty. We then finish with an exam style question.


EFL:

I tend to start with 5 minutes of light conversation to refresh our memory of the previous lesson. I then introduce the topic of the lesson, along with some real-world examples. I like to finish with free conversation in order to encourage speaking fluency.

Please note that these are rough guides and I will always adapt the lesson to suit your preferences. I also welcome any contribution to the structure or content of the lesson.
